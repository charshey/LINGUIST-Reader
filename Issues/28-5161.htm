Required Language(s): English (eng)
                            German (deu)

Description:

The Department of English and American Studies at Universität Kassel, Germany, invites applications for the following post (reference number: 31024): Lecturer (Lehrkraft für besondere Aufgaben, half-time position, E13 TV-L) for 12 months starting March 19, 2018.

For the official announcement follow this link: http://www.uni-kassel.de/intranet/aktuelles/stellenangebote/stellenausschreibung-details/tx_ukstellenausschreibung/31024.html

The successful applicant will be expected to teach linguistics courses (7 SWS per week, 1 SWS = 45 minutes) in the BA / MA programs at the English department. Language of instruction is English.

Specifications: Applicants should hold an MA (or equivalent degree) in English or General Linguistics. Advanced skills in English and German are necessary.

Preference will be given to candidates whose academic interest lies in the fields of theoretical linguistics (e.g., the morphology-syntax interface or lexical / conceptual semantics) and / or psycholinguistics (e.g., language comprehension or language acquisition) as well as the analysis of empirical data (corpus research, experimental investigation).

Application procedure: Applications should be sent by e-mail to the email address below or by postal mail to the University President of Universität Kassel, 34109 Kassel. Please quote the above reference number.

Women and candidates with disabilities are encouraged to apply. Universität Kassel is an equal opportunity employer.

Application Deadline: 05-Jan-2018

Email Address for Applications: bewerbungenuni-kassel.de

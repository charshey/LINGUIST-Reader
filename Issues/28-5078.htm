Summer Schools: 2nd Crete Summer School of Linguistics/Greece

Editor for this issue: Sarah Robinson <srobinsonlinguistlist.org>

Date: 03-Dec-2017
From: Vina Tsakali <tsakaliuoc.gr>
Subject: 2nd Crete Summer School of Linguistics/Greece
E-mail this message to a friend

Host Institution: University of Crete
Website: http://www.phl.uoc.gr/confs/cssl18/

Dates: 16-Jul-2018 - 27-Jul-2018
Location: Rethymnon, Crete, Greece

Focus: CreteLing 2018 focuses on Theoretical Linguistics (Syntax, Semantics, Phonology, Morphology, syntax-semantics interface, phonology-syntax interface), Sign Language, language evolution, animal communication and historical linguistics.
Minimum Education Level: Undergraduate

Description:
The Division of Linguistics at the University of Crete announces the 2nd Summer School in Linguistics. CreteLing2018 will take place from July 16 to July 27, 2018. There will be introductory courses and advanced seminars on syntax, phonology, sign language, morphology, semantics, syntax-semantics interface, phonology-syntax interface, language evolution, animal communication and historical linguistics. There will also be two workshops taking place within the CreteLing2018, which will be announced in January 2018.
The application form will be available online in mid January 2018 via the school’s website (http://www.phl.uoc.gr/confs/cssl18/). Please note that availability in the dorms and for attending is limited, so please consult our website for more information.

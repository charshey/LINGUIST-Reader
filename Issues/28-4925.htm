University or Organization: Erfurt University
Department: Linguistics
Job Location: Thuringia, Germany
Web Address: https://www.uni-erfurt.de/en/linguistics/
Job Title: Professor of Applied Linguistics (Psycholinguistics)

Job Rank: Professor

Specialty Areas: Applied Linguistics; Psycholinguistics


Description:

The Faculty of Philosophy at the University of Erfurt (Thuringia, Germany) invites applications for the following Professorship for the Winter Term 2018 / 2019: Professor (W2 level, with tenure track to W3 level) of Applied Linguistics with special focus on Psycholinguistics .

Duties:

The successful candidate will conduct their research and teaching in the area of Applied Linguistics in accordance with the teaching requirements: They will primarily offer relevant seminars for our MA Programme in Applied Linguistics: Acquisition, Processing and Use of Language; they will also, depending on their qualifications, contribute to teaching in our philological (i.e. language-based) programmes at the Faculty of Philosophy.

Requirements:

In addition to the general requirements for professorial posts, applicants need to fulfil the prerequisites that are laid down in §77 Thüringer Hochschulgesetz (Thuringian legal code for higher education).

Applicants are required to have a Ph.D. or other doctoral qualification in a relevant area for this post, and a Habilitation (second doctoral degree) or a comparably body of academic work, as well as excellent publications in the area of applied linguistics (focus: psycholinguistics). They will be expected to actively participate in postgraduate training offered in the research group “Sprachbeherrschung” which is part of one of the University’s main foci “education”. Successful applicants will further have relevant teaching experience and knowledge of the German language.

Additional Information:

The W2 level Professorship initially is available for a fixed-term period of 5 years. In accordance with the career track program at the University of Erfurt (https://www.uni-erfurt.de/forschung/karrierewege/erfurter-career-track/), it is planned that if the person appointed to the fixed term W2 professorship passes their evaluation at the end of their fixed-term W2 professorship, the W2 professorship will then be transformed into a permanent W3 level professorship.

The University of Erfurt strives to increase the number of women in its academic staff and therefore strongly encourages women to apply. Candidates with disabilities who have equal qualifications will be given preference.

The appointee is expected to relocate to the wider Erfurt area. The successful applicant is expected to contribute foundational, interdisciplinary courses to the “Studium Fundamentale”, which is partially taught in English.

Please note that only the German version of this job posting is legally binding. This version can be obtained here: https://www.uni-erfurt.de/personal-und-recht/ausschreibungen/

Application Procedure & Deadline:

Please submit your application including a cover letter, curriculum vitae, teaching and publication records, student evaluations, as well as copies of all relevant official documents and transcripts in electronic form (*.pdf) by 31st December 2017 stating the vacancy no 70/2017. Please also complete the application form which is available here: https://www.uni-erfurt.de/personal-und-recht/ausschreibungen/ (second page of the English / German call for applications pdf). Applications should be e-mailed to Universität Erfurt Philosophische Fakultät at the application email below.

Note:

The University of Erfurt regrets that it cannot reimburse any costs arising from the application process.

Application Deadline: 31-Dec-2017
